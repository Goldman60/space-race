Space Race

By: A.J. Fite - me@ajfite.com - CSC 471 Fall 2018

Project Description

Space Race is a relaxing single player space flight game. The player pilots the USS Enterprise (D) around a galaxy made up of 3D models from our project work this quarter. Including classics such as the Stanford Bunny, Utah Teapot, and the waving arm from the midterm. Players can also fly through a series of randomly generated rings to collect points.
